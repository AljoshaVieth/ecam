
EQUATIONS
=========

Desired authorized Consumption per capita (AUT_lpc_des)
Percent auth. consumption per capita GHG reduction (AUT_GHG_red%)

=========

Emissions								WS_GHG 			ws_KPI_GHG
Emissions per auth consumption volume	AUT_GHG_m3		WS_GHG / wsd_vol_auth
Emissions from auth consumption 		AUT_GHG			AUT_GHG_m3 * ws_SL_auth_con *ws_ser_pop *assessment period / 1000
Emissions for desired auth consumption	AUT_GHG_des		AUT_GHG_m3 * AUT_lpc_des * ws_ser_pop *assessment period / 1000
GHG reduction 							AUT_GHG_red%	(AUT_GHG - AUT_GHG_des)/WS_GHG*100
